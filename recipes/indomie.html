Indomie

Description

Indomie is a brand of instant noodle produced by the Indonesian company Indofood, the largest instant noodle manufacturer in the world with 16 factories. Over 28 billion packets of Indomie are produced annually, which are exported to more than 90 countries around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 Indomie noodle package (includes noodles, packet of soy sauce, chili sauce, and oil, and packet with seasoning powder and fried onion flakes)
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • Various vegetables (optional)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil water; when water comes to a boil add noodles for 3 minutes.
  2. If adding egg, fry egg on medium heat. If adding vegetables, chop vegetables.
  3. After 3 minutes are done, strain water from noodles and add all seasonings and sauces into noodles. Add any chopped vegetables and mix in and add egg on top if desired.
